Problem

Cities generate valuable public signals, but they are often fragmented and difficult to interpret quickly.

Solution

ProjectOne turns operational public data into a clear dashboard with KPIs, trends, and recent activity for faster situational awareness.

Social Impact

Social impact is the heart of the project. It follows an AI+H approach — AI for Humans, with Humans — where technology supports people instead of replacing them. The interface is architected for Universal Accessibility, with a roadmap for Eye-Tracking integration (Tobii) to empower operators with physical challenges.

City Story Layer

The platform is not only a monitoring tool. It is also a window into the living story of a city, revealing its rhythms, pressures, and needs through public data.

Safety, Privacy, and Auditability

As cities become more digital, public platforms must be built with mission-critical rigor. Safety, privacy, certification discipline, and audit readiness are important long-term principles for trustworthy digital city infrastructure.

Business Potential

ProjectOne can evolve into a lightweight municipal intelligence platform for operational awareness, service monitoring, and multi-city public data integration.

Future Roadmap

The next product layer is a feedback and customer-service module, designed to connect operational signals with direct user feedback and close the loop between city services and the community.

Built with AI+H

ProjectOne was built through a human-led AI+H process — AI for Humans, with Humans — combining human direction with support from multiple AI systems, including OpenAI ChatGPT, Google DeepMind tools, and Anthropic Claude, across ideation, refinement, and implementation.
